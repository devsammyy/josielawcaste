About us

Josie Lawcastle (JLC) is one of the youngest leading full service law firm in Nigeria, committed to providing zenith legal services in Nigeria, Africa, and globally while meeting the legal needs and demands of our esteemed clients.

The law firm comprises a team of vibrant,, innovative, and competent Legal Practitioners who have emerged in this 21st century to provide professional services with little to reservation. Thus, JLC is mindful of modern day demands and the diversity that comes with it.

JLC pioneered in January 2022 is headed and founded by Josephine Aransiola. Our legal team members are spread across the country and outside.

Our Mission

Providing legal solutions; attending to the legal demands of our clients with utmost regard to the precepts of excellence, integrity and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be one of the foremost leading legal services provider in Nigeria, and attain great success at all we do.
