Corporate Services

Services Offered:

  • Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) services
    • As an accredited CAC agent, Our sphere of services covers registration of businesses, incorporations, corporate searches, post incorporation matters, guidance and supports to startups and established companies alike to ensure regulatory compliance and procure statutory and other necessary data and documents.
  • Contract/legal document matters
    • We draft and review business contracts for clients; prepare legal documents for businesses etc
  • Obtainment of licenses
    • We help you get the license or certification your business needs such as SCUML, NSITF, Business permit, etc
  • Employment Onboarding books
    • We as well help develop all necessary onboarding douments and books your organization need for employees.
  • Notary Public services
    • We relief our clients the hassle of notarisation by assisting them get their documents notarized. Notarization, which can be related to oathing enjoys wider usage and recognition from various Authorities, and institution as it can be done on documents to be used outside the country.

Property Law Practice

We are proficient in offering clients real estate legal services that include conducting property due diligence and title investigation before transaction; preparation of land title documents, procuring necessary land documents and other necessary property documents for our clients; management of property etc

General Litigation

We provide legal advice and representation to individual and corporate organizations in disputes arising from their personal and business activities. We institute and defend different forms of legal action in all courts in Nigeria, including Contracts, Land Matters, Debt Recovery, Labour and Employment Matters, Human Rights Enforcement, Banking and Insurance Matters, Matrimonial Causes and Administration of Estate, Maritime and Aviation matters, among others. We advise clients and respond on their behalf to investigations by government agencies and regulators. We have extensive practice in the various High Courts in Nigeria.

Probate Services

We primarily focus on assisting Applicants in obtaining Letters of Administration for estates where the deceased passed away without a valid will (intestate). Our team ensures a smooth and efficient process, guiding clients through the legal requirements to manage and distribute the estate of their late loved ones in accordance with the law.
